Purpose

Site Name and Domain

The site is going to be called WDD Weather. The domain is: wddweather.com.

Site Purpose

WDD Weather will be a communtiy weather page for the fictional cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It will contain a page for each city, a storm center, and a gallery. The city pages will show current weather, 5-day forcasts, long range forecasts, and related weather news and information. It will also have a weather history, including record highs and lows. In the case of storms or other severe weather, warnings and suggested preparations/precautions will be posted.

The secondary purpose of this page is to be a student learning project for WDD230.
