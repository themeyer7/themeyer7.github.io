Web Site Audience

Target Audience

The target audience for WDD Weather includes the following:

Who: Citizens in the local areas, travelers coming to the areas, farmers, school children, parents, and others trying to plan activities that may be weather dependent

Age: 8-100

Income: $30,000 and up annually

Education: 4th grade and up

Technology: Personal computing devices, including, but not limited to, cell phones, tablets, and personal computers

Motivation: Find out what the current and/or future predicted weather is in the area, plan activities during favorable waether, prepare for inclement weather
